# Privacy Policy for DayCapture

**Last Updated: March 27, 2026**

## Overview

DayCapture is a daily personal journal app that allows users to capture their day through text and audio recordings. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

DayCapture does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data you create within the app — including text entries and audio recordings — is stored **locally on your device only**.

## Data Storage

- All text entries are stored in a local database on your device.
- All audio recordings are stored in the app's private internal storage on your device.
- No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.
- No internet connection is required to use the app.

## Third-Party Services

DayCapture does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app makes no network requests of any kind.

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

- **Microphone**: Required to record audio entries. Audio is stored locally and never transmitted.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by:

- Uninstalling the app, or
- Clearing the app data from your device settings.

## Children's Privacy

DayCapture does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, the app does not pose a privacy risk to any age group.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. If future versions of the app introduce any data collection or network features, this policy will be updated accordingly before those features are released.
